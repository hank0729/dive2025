About RiShima Dive

“Ri” comes from ancient Ryukyuan and means “ocean,” while “Shima” is the island cradled by cobalt‑blue seas.

When the waves whisper and breathe with the shore, RiShima Dive was quietly born—a hidden sanctuary for divers and a journey back to nature.

We guide you, in the gentlest way, to explore Okinawa’s clearest blues and most tranquil depths. Here you can dance with tropical fish in shafts of light and listen to your own dialogue with the ocean while gliding above coral gardens.

“Ryū‑Shima” is not only our name; it is an invitation: let every descent become your deepest connection with the sea.

The Ryukyuan ocean is where the story begins—and you will be its most captivating page.
